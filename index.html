PRAGATI ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Pragati Engineering College, Surampalem was established in 2001 by the Gayatri Educational Society The Institute is placed in an area of 25 acres of land giving wide academic and infrastructurals facilities. It is affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada, accredited by NAAC and approved by AICTE. It offers admissions at UG and PG levels with 1020 and 108 seatsssrespectively in Engineering stream.The full form of PEC Surampalem is PRAGATI ENGINEERING COLLEGE ,Surampalem. It offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in engineering streams.

***Events and Societies***

The college organizes a diverse range of events that cater to both entertainment and knowledge enrichment. Students can participate in engaging and fun-filled events such as Prism, Rangoli competitions, singing, storytelling, and more. Additionally, technical events focused on coding, idea representation, poster presentations and ppts and some fun quiz games, etc, and rewards are given based on performance. all these are conducted and managed by department students. These events provide opportunities for students to showcase their skills, learn from their peers, and enhance their overall abilities.

                            

                   

                                  

***PEC:CLUBS***

Clubs: PEC offers a variety of clubs catering to different interests, including gaming, cyber security, AR/VR, and more.
